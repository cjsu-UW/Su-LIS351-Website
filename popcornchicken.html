Taiwanese Popcorn Chicken

Ingredients:

Chicken Thigh:
Cut into small 1 inch chunks
Soy Sauce:
2 tbsp
Granulated Sugar:
2 tsp
Chinese Five Spice Powder:
1.5 tsp
Ground White Pepper:
1 tsp
Kosher Salt:
4 tsp
Vegetable Oil:
6 Cups
Sichuan Peppercorns:
1 tbps whole
Tapioca Flour:
1 Cup
Fresh Basil Leaves:
1 Cup

Instructions:

1:

Mix chicken thighs with sugar, soy, fives spice, white pepper and 2 tsp salt. Let marinate for 1 hr minimum.

2:

Heat oil in pot till 350 degrees. Grind sichuan pepper and 2 tsp salt for later.

3:

Batter chicken with tapioca flour and 1 tbsp water.

4:

Fry until golden brown. (5-7 minutes)

5:

Fry basil leaves for 1-2 minutes and mix with finished chicken.
